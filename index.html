About Pebbly Games

Simple ideas, carefully built.

Pebbly Games is a small independent game studio focused on creating playful and creative game experiences.

Gameplay comes first. Every project is designed so movement, timing, clarity, and atmosphere reinforce the same core idea instead of pulling in different directions.

We believe great games come from simple ideas executed with care. Our projects combine classic gameplay inspiration with fresh mechanics and charming worlds.

Our goal is to create games that feel fun, memorable, and full of personality.

How we design games

We focus on gameplay first. The best ideas are easy to understand, rewarding to master, and clear enough that every level, mechanic, and visual choice supports the same core feel.

  • Playful, readable core mechanics
  • Charming presentation with a clear identity
  • Classic inspiration combined with fresh ideas
  • Worlds players want to revisit

Built small enough to finish. Crafted carefully enough to last.
